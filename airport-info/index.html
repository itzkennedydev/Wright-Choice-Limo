O'Hare International & Midway International Arrivals

Please follow the following instructions when arriving at O’Hare and Midway Airport: 

  1. Whether you checked luggage or not, please proceed to the baggage claim level. 
  2. Once you have all your luggage, call 1-630-432-3937 to contact your driver.
  3. The driver will ask for your last name and/or confirmation number. Please have this information ready before you call. 
  4. The dispatcher will then tell you what door to go out. To help you find your vehicle, you will be given a description of it along with its license plate number. Please understand that O’hare regulations require livery vehicle to remain at holding lot until we have heard from the passenger, so it may take anywhere from 12-15 minutes for the car to reach you. However, if you are taking a shared ride, the vehicle may already be in the terminal picking up other passages, so pickup would be immediate. For this reason, it is important that you are absolutely ready to leave the airport when you call the assigned number and/or speak with the dispatcher.
